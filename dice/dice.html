Duncan’s Dice Game

How to play:
Type the number of players in the white box. Click the purple start button.
Then, clicking the purple roll button will roll the die for each successive player.
Every player’s total starts at 0, and when they roll the die the die’s value is added to their total,
unless they roll a one, at which point their total resets to zero.
The winner is the first player whose total equals or exceeds 20.

Please type how many players will be set!
